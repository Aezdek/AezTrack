Welcome to AezTrack
Quick guide — takes 30 seconds
1
Browse & open past papers
Go to Past Papers, pick a subject in Filters, and open any paper directly from the browser.
2
Log your marks in the tracker
The Dashboard shows a grid of all papers (2015–2025). Click any + cell to enter your marks.
3
Track grades with thresholds
When logging, grade boundaries are auto-fetched from the CIE PDF. The tracker then shows your grade (A*–G) instead of just a percentage.
4
Sync across devices (optional)
Click Cloud sync in the sidebar to back up your data. You'll get a passphrase — save it. No account needed.
Data is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is opt-in and requires your explicit consent.
